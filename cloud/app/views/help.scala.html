Build the IoT through a simple RESTful API

We provide a RESTful API for managing your things in a clean, clear, portable way.

POST /rest/things/register?url={thingUrl}
Register a Thing with and URL {thingUrl}. Returns the unique Thing identifier.
DELETE /rest/things/{thingId}
Delete Thing with identifier {thingId}

Resources discovery is simple. Register your thing by providing the URL hosting its unique identifier, e.g., http://{ip of your Thing}/id. Based on this identifier, SicsthSense will figure out how to use your Thing. If your Thing is running Contiki, for example, the full ist of its resources is available at /resources.
